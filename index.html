About

My current focus is post-training: how far reinforcement learning and distillation can push reasoning in language models. With the Computational Social Science Lab I co-developed ThinkTwice, which jointly optimizes models for reasoning and self-refinement, and helped distill master-level play into grounded reasoning — using chess as a fully verifiable domain for asking whether models actually reason, which is also why we built the ChessQA benchmark.

A second thread is language models for recommendation and information retrieval, from two years with Scott Sanner’s Data-Driven Decision Making Lab: elaborative query reformulation, Gaussian-process item scoring with LLM relevance judgments (Findings of ACL 2026), and manifold-aware metrics for dense passage retrieval (EMNLP 2025). At Air Canada I put some of this into practice and released TravelDest, the first benchmark dataset for travel search.

Agents tie the two together: systems that retrieve, decide, and act. SafeGEO studies how generative engine optimization can manipulate recommendation agents, and at ZBot Technology, which I co-founded, I lead development of an export-oriented AI agent system. Earlier I worked on decision-making for autonomous driving at Bosch.

Research

Post-training for reasoning

How far can reinforcement learning and on-policy distillation push reasoning in language models? Joint optimization for reasoning and self-refinement, distilling expert play into grounded reasoning, and benchmarks that make progress measurable.

ThinkTwice Master Distillation ChessQA

Language-model agents

Agents retrieve, decide, and act — and can be manipulated. SafeGEO maps how generative engine optimization distorts what recommendation agents surface, and at ZBot Technology I build agent systems that ship.

SafeGEO

Recommendation & retrieval

Language models change how people search for things they cannot name yet. Elaborative query reformulation, Gaussian-process item scoring with LLM relevance judgments, and manifold-aware metrics for dense passage retrieval.
